After that was the Solid team meeting, and the focus was on the diversity and inclusion initiative Marrelle is spearheading. She suggested that we include more HBCUs in the Solid ecosystem and I said I'd send some emails and see if we can get connected to Morehouse and Spelman.

After that was a very long meeting with the founder of Graphmetrix where I finally got technical explanations for what they're doing. They're structuring all data as the result of events in an event-based database design. He was also interested in Liqid Chat to embed in their own UI. I said that while I can't implement features specifically for them, I could build some general features that would allow them to embed it.

Finally I synced up with Danny Done who is working on raising money for his HR related Solid Startup. I think it was really productive and we started making a vision around what how his HR vision works into the data browser. So, given I get funding, we have an app developer hahaha!
