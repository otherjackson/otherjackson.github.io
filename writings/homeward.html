Homeward

This is the pilot script for a TV show called Homeward. It's my attempt to write something in the "grimdark" genre.

In a dystopian future, humanity has not only discovered that hell is real and can be visited, but that everyone, no matter how good or evil they were, goes there. A thriving industry of "Rescue Services" makes money by rescuing the deceased wealthy from Hell while using the less fortunate in society to do the dirty work.

I stopped writing right before the protagonist entered hell because I think I would need to enlist the mind of someone more twisted to come up with what Hell is like.
